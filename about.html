About Us

The New Standard is a design studio that brings together community and collaboration, aiming to amplify the natural networking that occurs within a shared space. It serves as a global showcase where Asian art, craftsmanship, and design excellence are displayed to the world.

We offer a platform for the communication, exposure, and presentation of both historical and iconic furniture designers, as well as contemporary design brands and emerging talents. Each exhibitor contributes their unique story to a unified narrative, collectively shaping a new and significant chapter in the history of Asian design.

Our Showroom

Commanding three floors and over 11,000 square feet, our immersive, multi-level design destination features artistic installations of home furnishings in a gallery setting. The gallery seamlessly integrates The New Standard Kitchens, Dining, Living and Bedroom collections with rare art, artifacts and home complements from across Asia. The New Standard also includes our own Design Studio, where you can work with our professional designers to reimagine your space and conceptualize a fresh and inspiring way to live.

Connect With Us

Partnering with us means more than just sourcing furniture; it’s about co-creating a cohesive design narrative that reflects your vision. We offer a wide range of furniture systems, meticulously designed to complement and enrich your design concepts. Together, we can transform spaces into harmonious environments that inspire and endure.

Let’s work together to bring your design ideas to life with furniture solutions that perfectly align with your creative goals. Reach out to explore the possibilities and start a conversation on how we can collaborate to push the boundaries of design.
