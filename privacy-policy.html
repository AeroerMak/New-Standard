Privacy Policy | Date : 26 May 2025

At The New Standard, accessible from Thenewstandard.my, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect  

We may collect the following types of information :

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number, or any other details you submit through our contact forms or subscriptions.

Non-Personal Information

Browser type, device information, pages visited, and time spent on the site, collected via cookies and analytics tools.

01  

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to :

  • Respond to your inquiries or service requests.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Send you updates or promotional content (if you opt in).
